About Us

ArtDatabase seeks to create great transparency of the art world and markets, giving users tools for research, investments and much more. We have implemented the most advanced databasing and tracking software in fine art, as our constantly growing database stays up to date with the current movements of fine art. Whether you are a casual browser or a fine art researcher, ArtDatabase provides the software that you need to explore the art world.

ArtDatabase was created in 2018 by John Kimball, an undergraduate student at Brown University. The foundation of ArtDatabase started with the concept of a highly centralized database of all fine art. With our centralized database, we are able to implement new tools for research involving artificial intelligence, machine learning, market analytics and collection management.


We are constantly exploring the different ways that we can involve new technology to enhance the research and outreach of fine art all over the world.

Our Team

Our dedicated team has been with us since the very start of ArtDatabase, and have made it into what it is today. Led by Founder and CEO, John Kimball, our team has pushed the boundaries of data analysis and technology within fine art. We are primarily located in New York City, but our team is located all over the world in London, Providence, Venezuela and Toronto.
